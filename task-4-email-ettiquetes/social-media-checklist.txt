#Do's

1.Keep your profiles professional — use a decent profile photo and a clear bio
2.Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with your professor or future employer seeing it
3.Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts
4.Share your achievements, projects, and learning milestones to build a positive online presence
5.Give credit when you share someone else's work, ideas, or content

#Don'ts

1.Don't post negative comments about your college, professors, or classmates publicly
2.Don't share personal information like your phone number, address, or daily location
3.Don't engage with or spread unverified news, rumours, or fake information
4.Don't use social media during lectures or important academic activities
5.Don't accept friend/follow requests from people you don't know or trust
