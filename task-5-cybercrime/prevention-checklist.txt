#Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1.Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
2.Do not approve unknown “collect requests”
3.Always verify the identity of the sender before making payments
4.Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
5.Use only official and trusted payment apps
6.Enable app lock and two-factor authentication
7.Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
UPI-Specific Safety Tips:
8.Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money
9.Double-check payment requests before approving

Reporting:
Report cyber fraud at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
Call helpline: 1930 (24×7)
